Education

Do you want to have a great future?

I hope I have caugth your attention and I promise not to disappoint. I will be direct, you must study daily no matter where you come from or your financial situation. If you want to have a promising future there is no magic formula, you must study, study and study. You should never give up, this is a must.

Platzi

For this reason I love Platzi's slogan "Never stop learning" and this is the first resource I recommend. An online education platform that gives you access to many careers, from English School to Web Development School, Digital Journalism School, Graphic Design School and many more.

I am currently studying at Platzi and I am focused on web development and English. I highly recommend it and I advise you that to give your best you should always try to be self-taught, only so you can achieve your goals.
