Honduras: vaccination and corruption

To begin with, I want to make a small comparison with our sister country El Savalvador that fully exposes what is happening in our country. So far, Honduras has a total of 57,639 doses administered, of which only 2,639 have received a second dose. Which leads to a figure of least than 0.1% of the vaccinated population.

El Salvador has a total of 991,000 doses administered, of which 101,000 have received a second dose. Which leads to a figure of 1.6% of the vaccinated population.

The two hurricanes of last year plus the pandemic and the lack of care of the population have plunged our country down. All this without counting the lack of government organization, low level of problem solving and hight levels of corruption to the utmost.

Despite everything, I encourage the Honduran population to fight not only for their future but also to idealize a better future for our country and thus be able to work towards those dreams.
