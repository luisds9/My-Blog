Honduras: videogames and education

When I was young it was very common that in my school class we had two or three classmates that they didn't study. Of course, we played videogames in our free time.

Now in my country, children prefer spend more time playing videogames than studying. The must of their don't do their homework and don't like to read.

They love playing Free Fire all day, every single day. Nowadays, there are more number of children on a class that don't study. This is worrying because this minds are the future of our nation. If this situation doesn't change I think darker times are coming for Honduras.
